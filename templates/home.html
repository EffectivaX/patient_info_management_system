About Us

We have dedicated our time and work to the creation of a working of a prototype that is both efficient and cost effeffective, easy to use and very much modern. The fundamental basis being the provision of health care services to the Municipality of the beautiful town of Victoria Falls.

Problem Statement.

The Victoria Falls Municipality is in dire need of modern and efficient systems that they can adopt in order to become The Smart City it seeks to become by the year 2030. The municipality is in need of recording and reporting systems in order to be knowledgeable about the city's health concern on all of the local residents and also for the tourists that flocmk the amazing town of Victoria Falls

To solve this problem we have come up with technologically based solutions that work hand in love with each other. We have a web app that is used by the administrators in hospitals, clinics and private practictioners mainly for collecting the data they may have on the patients that come to visit them, the ordinary people can also use the web platform to make appointments with doctors, to check on the events being carried out by the town's health department and more, however they cannot themselves upload any medical inofrmation of their own however there will be a section received for any anomalies that residents may want to report.

Furthermore we have a USSD option which is accessed by dialing *224#, the USSD option seeks to serve the people that are not always connectedvto the internet whenever they need health or emergency services. Our USSD option has a rich functionality that includes making direct calls to emergency services including the Police, Ambulances Services, Fire Brigade, Wildlife Monitors and Fault Reporting.
